Our facilities

Library

Dr. Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan Learning Resources Centre, the central Library has a floor area of 2977 Sq.Mtr. with a reading space 770 Sq.Mtr. It houses more than 13,173 titles, 1,17,330 volumes (includes rare books 123 titles) and subscribes to 24985 E-Journals, 97608 E-Books and also 83 National and International print Journals & magazines. It enables students to access remotely to IEEE IEL, ASME, ASCE, ELSEVIER, J-GATE e-journals, NList, DELNET and NDLI.


Cafeteria

college cafeteria is a place where students can go to eat a variety of meals and snacks. The cafeteria have a seating area where students can sit and eat their meals or hang out with friends


Sports

The college has been actively encouraging students to participate in various sports & games as it believes that there should be holistic development of an individual. The Department of Physical Education conducts sports and games for the staff and students viz., Cricket, Football etc. Our students have excelled and won prizes in various intra-college, inter-college and university competitions.
